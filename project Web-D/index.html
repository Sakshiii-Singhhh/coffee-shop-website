ABOUT US

Café Coffee Day Global Limited Company is a Chikkamagaluru-based business which grows coffee in its own estates of 20,000 acres.It is the largest producer of arabica beans in Asia, exporting to various countries including U.S., Europe, and Japan. V. G. Siddhartha started the café chain in 1996 when he incorporated Coffee Day Global, which is the parent of the Coffee Day chain.
